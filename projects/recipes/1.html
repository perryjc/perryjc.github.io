Campfire salmon

Tools

  • Cleaning Knife
  • Campfire
  • Cast iron grill or metal cooking grate
  • Metal or wood tongs (NOT Plastic or silicone)

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Salt and/or other seasonings
  • Any Citrus (lime is my favourite but orange has an interesting flavor)

Steps

  1. Clean salmon (skip if already clean)
    • Insert knife in the belly near the tail
    • Cut from tail to head (don't push deep or you will puncture the intestines)
    • Remove head
    • Remove tail
    • Open the fish along the cut line
    • Scoop out organs with your hands
    • Rinse inside with clean water
    • Cut the spine of the fish from the belly out
    • Remove skin and bones
    • You should be left with 2 fillets
  2. Season fish however you like (only add citrus first if you are useing orange)
  3. Place fillets on fire
  4. Cook about 3-5 minutes on each side (until slightly blackend)
  5. Serve and apply citrus
