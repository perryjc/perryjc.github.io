Raw oyster

Tools

  • Shucking knife
  • Cloth or leather for thigh (A towel works fine)
  • Cut resistant work gloves
  • Oyster fork

Ingredients

  • Fresh oysters (Not more than 24 hours after caught)
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Shuck oysters
    1. Grasp oyster firmly in non dominant hand
    2. With dominant hand, press knife into the back "hinge" of oyster
    3. Press firmly and twist blade
    4. Once "hinge" releases, slide blade along the inside of the oyster, twisting till you reach the front
    5. Remove and dispose of top shell
    6. Repeat intill all are shucked
  2. Plate oysters
  3. Squeeze lemon to taste (or about a quater lemon per half dozen
  4. Serve and enjoy
